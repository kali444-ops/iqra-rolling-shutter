About Us

IQRA ROLLING SHUTTER

Iqra Rolling Shutter is a trusted rolling shutter manufacturer and service provider in Pune, Maharashtra, delivering high-quality shutter solutions for shops, warehouses, factories, and commercial properties at affordable prices.

We specialize in designing, fabricating, and installing manual and motorized rolling shutters based on customer requirements. Our services include shop shutter installation, industrial shutter fitting, and shutter repair and maintenance. Every project is completed with accurate measurement, secure installation, and a strong focus on durability and safety.

With quality materials and skilled workmanship, we provide long-lasting and smooth-operating rolling shutters that improve security and performance for residential and commercial spaces across Pune.
