About Us

Iqra Rolling Shutter is a trusted rolling shutter manufacturer and service provider offering high-quality shutter solutions for shops, warehouses, and commercial properties. We focus on strong materials, precise fitting, and long-lasting performance to ensure better security and smooth operation at affordable prices.

Our Work

We design, fabricate, and install manual and motorized rolling shutters based on customer requirements. Our work includes shop shutter installation, industrial shutter fitting, and shutter repair and maintenance. Every project is completed with proper measurement, secure installation, and attention to durability and safety.
